## 1. Customers & dealers

Maximize profit by keeping high-spenders for yourself and assigning low-budget customers to NPC dealers.

<a id="11-golden-rules"></a>
### 1.1 Golden rules

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| **VIPs for you, junkies for dealers** | Dealers take a **20% cut** and only sell at market value. You can **counter-offer** and upcharge high-rollers for much higher margins. |
| **Personal sales = 20 XP; dealer sales = 10 XP** | Early game (below Hoodlum V): give dealers only **4 of 8 slots** so you keep more XP. Use dealers more once you’ve ranked up. |
| **Counter-offer everything** | When selling to Uptown VIPs yourself, always counter-offer to push quantity and price higher. |
| **4 AM trick** | After 4:00 AM, time stops. Visit customers at their houses for extra sales and big XP gains. |

<a id="15-pro-tips-summary"></a>
### 1.5 Pro tips summary

1. **XP:** Below Hoodlum V → give each dealer **4 slots** (not 8). More personal deals = more XP (20 vs 10).
2. **Leo:** Use for overflow and, later, $10k Uptown customers. Never give him your $12k Uptown whales.
3. **Wei:** Don’t load him with high-value customers until Suburbia Cartel Influence is reduced.
4. **Product:** Use **1.4 pricing multiplier** and high-quality (e.g. Purple/Gold) mixes where it matters; pair strong product with dealers so even “minnows” pay well.
5. **Risk:** When cartel influence is high, you and dealers can get hit; keep your best customers and best product on your own runs and don’t over-stack dealers with whales.

## 2. Efficiency & mega rich

Daily efficiency, progression, XP, money, properties, best recipe, profit by type, end-game checklist, and links.

<a id="21-daily-efficiency--how-to-play-each-day"></a>
### 2.1 Daily efficiency — how to play each day

- **Morning (after 4 AM):** XP is tallied when you go to bed, so each day is one “run.” Start by doing **unplanned deals in person** before NPCs contact your dealers — personal deals give **20–30 XP** each; dealer deals only **10 XP**. Hit your **whales and high-multiplier customers** first (see [Keep for self](#keep-for-self-your-personal-list-only) list).
- **Counter-offer every Uptown sale** — push quantity and price; never accept first offer on VIPs.
- **Curfew (9 PM–5 AM):** Sales during curfew get a **~20% cash bonus**. Plan big deliveries for this window when safe.
- **4 AM trick:** After **4:00 AM the clock stops**. You can visit customers at their houses and stack more deals in one “day” for huge XP and cash before sleeping.
- **Launder daily:** Hit your laundering businesses (Laundromat, Car Wash, etc.) so dirty cash becomes clean; ATMs have limits and clean money is needed for some purchases.
- **Don’t over-assign dealers early:** Below Hoodlum V, give dealers only **4 slots each** so more deals come to you (more XP). Scale dealers up once you’re ranking is set.

### 2.3 Rank / XP — level up as fast as possible

- **Best XP:** Make **as many in-person deals as possible**, even small ones (1–2 product, low price). Each deal ≈ **20–30 XP**; dealer deal = **10 XP**. Use **unplanned deals** (walk up, “Offer them a deal”) early in the day.
- **Product type matters:** Basic weed can net **~500 XP/day**; Meth and better products can push **~1000 XP/day** with in-person sales.
- **Extra XP:** Harvest/trim plants **~10 XP**; kill cops/cartel **~20 XP** each; escape police (Wanted) **~40 XP** once per day; pickpocket **~5 XP**.
- **4 AM freeze:** Use the frozen clock to do extra house calls and stack deals before bed so that day’s XP is maximized.

**Reference:** [How To Rank Up Fast (The Gamer)](https://thegamer.com/schedule-1-level-up-fast-exp-farm-rank-up-quick-how-to-guide) · [Best Ways to Get XP (Game Rant)](https://gamerant.com/schedule-1-how-level-up-get-xp-fast)

### 2.4 Money maximization — get mega rich

- **Pricing:** Always charge **above** the customer’s offer (even **+$30** or more); use **counter-offer** on every Uptown VIP.
- **Product:** Move from weed to **cocaine/meth** and **high-value mixes** as soon as you unlock them. One top mix ([2.6 Best recipe](#26-best-recipe-most-profitable-mix--end-game-cash)) is **~$693 profit per batch**.
- **Curfew bonus:** Sell during **9 PM–5 AM** for **~20% extra cash** when you can do it safely.
- **4 AM trick:** More deals in one “day” = more cash and XP before sleep.
- **Casino (optional):** East of Taco Ticklers; open **4 PM–5 AM**. **Blackjack** or **slots** (three 7s = **×100** bet). Save before big bets and reload on loss to multiply clean cash.
- **Trash / machines:** Trash for Cash + Trash Grabber; smash ATMs/vending (baseball bat) for quick cash — avoid machines near your bases and usual stores.
- **Reduce cartel influence:** Gain customers, win ambushes, interrupt dealer ops, spray graffiti, take out cartel dealers so your operations (and dealers) aren’t hit.

**Reference:** [How To Make Money (The Gamer)](https://thegamer.com/schedule-1-how-to-make-money-fast-guide) · [Best Recipe (Game Rant)](https://gamerant.com/schedule-1-best-recipe-high-value-mix-most-profit)

### 2.5 Properties & money laundering — order and end game

**Laundering:** Dirty cash must be cleaned to avoid ATM limits and to buy some properties. Buy businesses and run them daily.

| Business | Price | Daily laundry |
|----------|-------|----------------|
| Laundromat | $4,000 | $2,000 |
| Post Office | $10,000 | $4,000 |
| Car Wash | $20,000 | $6,000 |
| Taco Ticklers | $50,000 | $8,000 |

**Suggested order:** Laundromat first (cheapest, central). Then Car Wash (best value for $20k). Post Office if you want a Downtown spot. Taco Ticklers last for **max daily laundry** when you’re mega rich.

**Production properties:**

| Property | Price | Employees | Notes |
|----------|-------|-----------|--------|
| Bungalow | $6,000 | 5 | Best value; Westville; good for Shrooms/meth/cannabis. |
| Barn | $25,000 | 10 | Half the cost of Warehouse; Suburbia; need a car. |
| Docks Warehouse | $50,000 | 10 | Central; best for huge automated setups + Shroom farm. |

**End-game:** Own **Docks Warehouse** (or Barn) for production, **all four laundering businesses** for max daily clean cash, and use the [foolproof dealer + personal list](#16-foolproof-late-game-setup-all-customers-all-dealers) so you only run 6–8 VIPs yourself.

<a id="33-manual-growing-step-by-step-best-result"></a>
### 3.3 Manual growing (step-by-step, best result)

Do this once so you understand the cycle; then automate with employees.

1. **Spawn:** Put **Mushroom Spawn Station** in your hideout. Interact → put **1 Grain Bag** and **1 Spore Syringe** in the slots. Remove syringe cap, inject into the grain bag through the **black square**. Use **A / D** to tilt the syringe, then push the plunger. Wait until **Shroom Spawn** is produced.
2. **Box:** Place the **Mushroom Box** (Mushroom Bed) **near the Spawn Station**. Add **Mushroom Substrate** to the box (like filling soil). Put the fresh **Shroom Spawn** in the box and break it into **16 chunks**, spread evenly **under** the substrate.
3. **Temperature:** Put an **AC Unit** in the same room, next to the bed. Set it to **Cold** (cool mode). Mushrooms need cool conditions (below ~15°C) to fruit properly.
4. **Water:** Fill the **Spray Bottle** at any tap. Spray the Mushroom Box — hit **five different spots**. **One full spray = +56% growth**; **two full sprays = full growth**. Don’t skip the second spray if you want the best yield.
5. **Harvest:** When fully grown, click the mushrooms to harvest. Store in **sachets or baggies** before selling.

**Reference:** [Schedule 1 Mushrooms – How to Get & Growing Setup (gamer.org)](https://www.gamer.org/schedule-1-mushrooms-how-to-get-growing-setup-guide/) · [How to Get and Grow Mushrooms – Prima Games](https://primagames.com/tips/how-to-get-and-grow-mushrooms-in-schedule-1-shrooms-update-guide)

<a id="35-full-automation-best-possibility"></a>
### 3.5 Full automation (best possibility)

To run Shrooms with **no micromanaging**, you need the right gear plus staff and clipboard links.

#### Minimum for a simple two-ingredient Shroom mix

- **1× Mushroom Spawn Station** · **1× Mushroom Bed** · **1× Mixing Station** · **1× Packaging Station**
- **Drying Racks** (recommended: **1 per Mushroom Bed**) — set quality to **yellow star (Heavenly)** for best product.
- **AC Unit** next to the bed, set to **Cold**.

#### Staff (hire from Manny)

- **1× Botanist** — Spawn Station, Drying Rack, Mushroom Bed. Supply rack: Mushroom Substrate + Shroom Spawn (or Grain Bag + Spore Syringe).
- **1× Handler** — Routes: (1) Baggies / finished product rack ↔ Packaging Station, (2) Ingredient rack → Mixing Station.
- **1× Chemist** — Assign to Mixing Station (required once you add any Mixing Station, or production stops).
- **Optional: 1× Cleaner** — Assign to Trash Can(s) near beds; add Trash Cans from Dan. Give Cleaner a bed/locker.

**Rule:** Each **Mixing Station** needs a **Chemist** (one Chemist can run up to four stations). Pay all employees: put **cash in their briefcase** on their bed/locker. They work **4am–7am**.

#### Clipboard setup (automation links)

- **Mushroom Bed:** (Optional) add Fertilizer to speed growth; not required if using Drying Racks.
- **Drying Rack:** Set **Destination** = Mixing Station (or next Drying Rack if chained). Set **Quality** = **Heavenly** (yellow star).
- **Mixing Station:** Set **Destination** = next Mixing Station (multi-ingredient) or **Packaging Station** (two-ingredient mix).
- **Packaging Station:** Set **Destination** = the same Storage Rack that holds Baggies (keeps finished product and packaging together).

Scale up: more Beds, more Drying Racks (one per Bed), more Mixing Stations (one Chemist per station or shared across up to 4), and a second Spawn Station. Point all Drying Racks to the first Mixing Station; chain Mixing Stations by destination.
